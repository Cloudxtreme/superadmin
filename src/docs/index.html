Hotfixes we really, maniacally have to test the implications of the update on:

1) other parts of the platform
2) the different networks (if post-related)
3) the 3 major browsers

There is no staging (testing) for hotfixes. If we fail on any of the 3 checks, our users notice it immediatly - that's disastrous.
