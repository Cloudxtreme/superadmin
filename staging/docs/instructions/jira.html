Jira

Small Mantra

Minor Bugs

Minor bugs should be fixed somewhere within 2 days, maximum a week, depending on the content.

Major Bugs

Major bug tickets always have priority when you start your day and come before the start of a new task if the day already started.

Critical tickets

Blocking and critical have the same urgency, with the only difference that blocking tickets come before critical ones.
(if working on a critical ticket, you drop it for a blocking one)

Blocking tickets

Blocking bug tickets come before anything, including your work in progress.
